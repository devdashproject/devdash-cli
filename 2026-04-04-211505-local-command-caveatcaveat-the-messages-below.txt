вҸә dd diagnose <id> is an investigative command that gives you a consolidated
  view of a bead (issue) and its associated jobs. Here's what it does:

  1. Fetches the bead and prints a summary line: short ID, subject, status,
  priority, and type.
  2. Lists all jobs associated with that bead вҖ” each with a status icon, short
  ID, status, and creation timestamp.
  3. Shows failure details for the first failed job it finds:
    - The error message
    - A failure analysis summary (if available)
    - The last 30 lines of the job's output log

  It's essentially a one-stop "what happened?" command вҖ” useful when a bead's
  jobs aren't completing as expected and you want to see the status, history,
  and any error details without making multiple API calls manually.
